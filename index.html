codeMath is an initiative that seeks to explore new ways of teaching Math through Code. It seeks to find new approaches to two topics that are non-intuitive.

trajectory

This is a long term project with different stages. The most immediate step is to show the potential and establish relationships between Code and Math. This will serve as a precedent to start building curriculum as a new way to teach.

framework

The following examples showcase how Code Interacts with Math to illustrate relationships and build upon a goal. 12 mini lessons that walk the student from Variables to graphing in the Cartisean Plane. Giving the opportunity to build knowledge from lessons to lessons.

3. Cartesian Plane

Let's put aside our concepts of variables and equations which we will bring later and focus on the Cartesian plane.
The Cartesian plane or Coordinate plane is a canvas that has two axes, one vertical and one horizontal, X and Y respectively. Through the use of Cartesian coordinates we can position elements on the canvas.
